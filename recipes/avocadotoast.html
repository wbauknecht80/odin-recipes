Avocado Toast

Description:

Avocado Toast: or in this case, more specifically, avocado toast with an over easy egg on top. While optional, the egg is what makes our own version so special.

Ingredients:

  • Half Avocado
  • Bread of Choice
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Tajin
  • Chili Flakes

Directions:

  1. Toast bread in toaster.
  2. Begin heating a large amount of butter in a small frying pan.
  3. When toast is done, butter one side; follow with avocado, then tajin and chili flakes.
  4. When pan is thoroughly lubricated and heated, crack egg into it. Do not let the yolk break on this step!!
  5. Season egg with salt and pepper.
  6. Once the egg no longer sticks to the pan, flip it like the bloody legend that you are.
  7. Allow to cook for about 30 seconds more before removing from heat.
  8. Promoptly put the egg on your awaiting toast and enjoy the fruits of your labor.
