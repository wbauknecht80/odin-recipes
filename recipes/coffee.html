Pour Over Coffee

Description:

Pour Over Coffee: the most based beverage on the Earth. Is it a meal? Inherently, no. But lord knows you can make a zero calorie beverage a few hundred, maybe even thousand, if you try hard enough.

Ingredients:

  • Coffee Beans, Medium Grind
  • Pour Over Kit and Filter
  • Hot Water
  • Your Favorite Mug

Directions:

  1. Put the filter in the top of your pourver kit.
  2. Put the ground beans into the filter, and put the kit on top of your mug.
  3. Allow the coffee to be born into this world.
  4. Drink your coffee--savor this moment, for there are few quite like it in this tumultuous world.
