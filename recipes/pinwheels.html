Steak and Spinach Pinwheels

Description:

Steak and Spinach Pinwheels: butterflied flank steak stuffed with spinach, garlic, bacon and parmesan cheese. Best served with roasted brocolli and angel hair tossed in garlic and olive oil.

Ingredients:

  • Flank Steak
  • Spinach
  • Garlic
  • Bacon
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • ~6" Wooden Skewers

Directions:

  1. Butterfly the steaks so they lie flat and are roughly the same thickness throughout; season with salt and pepper and set aside.
  2. Finely chop the spinach and garlic; combine in bowl and mix thoroughly.
  3. Shred parmesan cheese into mixture, and stir once more.
  4. Cover steaks with even coating of spinach/garlic/cheese mixture.
  5. Lie bacon flat on top of mixture, all parallel to one another.
  6. Roll steaks up in direction bacon is lying; once rolled, insert wooden skewers at equal distance from one another.
  7. Cut steak into even pieces.
  8. Broil on high for 10-15 minutes, flipping half-way through.
  9. Let rest for 5 minutes before eating.
